Robin Mesnage

CV

I manage a toxicology research programme at King’s College London. My research focus over the last 10 years has been on the safety evaluation of environmental contaminants, including pesticides, publishing more than 60 scientific articles on this subject. These publications had a substantial impact and were cited more than 4,500 times (h-index = 28). When not working on research projects at King’s College London, I provide expert consulting services and work as a scientific Editor.

PROFESSIONAL EXPERIENCE

I am scientific consultant for the Buchinger Wilhelmi clinic, which specialises in therapeutic fasting. My role is to provide support to deliver research objectives – from the conception of the studies, the realisation of experiments and the analysis of their results, to the drafting of manuscripts and their publications in peer-reviewed journals.
